Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, click the "Apply for Loan" button from the home page or navigation menu. Fill out the loan application form with your personal information, loan amount, interest rate, and term. You can also use our loan calculator to determine the right loan terms for your needs.

What documents do I need to apply?

For loan applications, you'll typically need:

  • Valid government-issued ID (driver's license or passport)
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Bank account information for loan disbursement

How is interest calculated?

Interest is calculated using the formula: Principal × Rate × Time. For example, a $10,000 loan at 5% interest for 1 year would accrue $500 in interest. Our loan calculator can help you understand the total cost of your loan including interest and fees.

How do I make loan payments?

You can make loan payments through:

  • Online payment portal in your dashboard
  • Bank transfer to the account provided
  • Automatic payments (set up in settings)
  • Mail (check or money order)

All payments are recorded in your loan history and dashboard.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If you miss a payment, you'll receive a notification via email and SMS (if enabled). Late fees may apply after the grace period. Contact us immediately if you're having trouble making a payment - we may be able to work out a payment plan or extension.

How do I view my loan history?

You can view your complete loan history by clicking on the "History" link in the navigation menu. This page shows all your loans, their status, payment history, and allows you to export your data. You can filter by status, year, or search by borrower name.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect all data transmission. Your personal and financial information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by clicking on the "Profile" link in the navigation menu. From there, you can edit your personal details, contact information, and preferences. Changes are saved automatically to your local browser storage.

How do I use the loan calculator?

The loan calculator helps you understand loan costs before applying. Enter the loan amount, interest rate, and term length. The calculator will show your monthly payment, total interest, and total cost. You can also view an amortization schedule and export the data for your records.

Can I export my loan data?

Yes, you can export your loan data in several ways:

  • From the History page: Export complete loan history as CSV
  • From the Reports page: Generate detailed financial reports
  • From the Calculator: Export amortization schedules
  • From individual loan details: Print or save loan information
